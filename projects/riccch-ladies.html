Riccch Ladies

duration my role team deliveries
3 Months - March to April of 2019​​​ Design Student Lucas Araujo Moodboards, Patterns and Digital clothing

A design print project based on three fictional characters: Cassandra, Cecilia and Conceição, all named with the letter c. Many studies about print techniques were made, besides some applications that consider three different collor palletes, enhancing the triad of the “Ricccas”.

Cecilia - The youngest rich persona

Cecília was conceive to be a young-adult woman full of attitude and with a edgy refined fashion style made of many contrasts. Thus, I utilized celebrities like Katy Perry, Natalia Kills and Andrey Hepburn as fashion references. All clothes were designed strictely in black-and-white to highligh the opositions in the prints made to the character.

Cassandra - The second rich persona

Cassandra was conceive to be a rich old lady that loves the color pink and floral patterns. Thus, I utilized a character with the same name, Cassandra, from the brazilian TV series "Sai de Baixo" as the main fashion reference to design the floral print.

Conceição - The oldest rich persona

Cassandra was conceive to be a rich old lady that loves the color pink and floral patterns. Thus, I utilized a character with the same name, Cassandra, from the brazilian TV series "Sai de Baixo" as the main fashion reference to design the floral print.
