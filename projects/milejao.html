Bom é ser feliz com MileJão

duration my role team deliveries
4 Months – Aug/18 to Dec/18 Branding Designer Bárbara Sueiro, Sofia Franco, Felipe de Castro e Lucas Araujo Visual Identity and documentation

This book synthesizes what happiness is for Milena and João (MileJão), two college friends of mine. Introspection, intelectuality and creativity are the key-words that represents perfectly what bring them joy. This project is inspired by "Wreck this journal" from Keri Smith and "Use This If You Want to Take Great Photographs" from Henri Carrol.

Visual Identity Concept

The content of the book demands active participation from the reader. Thus, It is impressed only in black-and-white so It can be colored by the will of the owner of the book. The bookbinding was made in a simple hotmelt to assure the durability necessary to many manipulations the book is going to suffer.
