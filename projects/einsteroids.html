Einsteroid

duration my role team deliveries
1 Month – November to December of 2020 UI Designer João Pedro Mafra, Fernanda Netto, Lucas Araujo e Lucas Frazão Videogame

Einsteroid is an arcade game in which you must help Einstein, the astronaut, to survive in space for as long as possible. You'll need to use your gravitational skills to dodge all the meteors, satellites and all the flying saucers that come in your way while floating in space. Test your reflexes with simple controls: change gravity with only one button! How long can you survive?!
