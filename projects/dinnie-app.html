Dinnie - Feed the net

duration my role team deliveries
5 Months - Aug/19 to Dec/19 Designer and Researcher Bárbara Sueiro, Juliana Helena Santiago, Léon de Carvalho, Lucas Araujo and Sofia Franco App, research and documentation

Dinnie is an interface that promotes interaction between people, allowing them to find and create content about alimentation through the construction of a supportive network.

App Personas

To all ages, to all diets. We projected Cláudia and Cléber as personas that group characteristics from our target audience: a person that is conscious about the environmental impact that their eating behaviour causes. Wanna have a tasty and sustainable diet?! Come to Dinnie!

User Interface

With Dinnie, you can read, write, and share content within themed groups — and even push it out to other platforms. Groups support all kinds of media: audio, video, photos, text, and quotes. When someone signs up, they take a quick profile quiz with questions about their eating habits and goals for behavior change. Based on that, Dinnie personalizes the experience using an algorithm that recommends content that’s been relevant to people with similar profiles.
