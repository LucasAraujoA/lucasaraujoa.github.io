With this innovative technique, use your eyebrows and mouth to move the character, collecting points by getting all the items that you can! Play to become the best apprentice that Cuca ever had. This is Cuca Catch!

Game trailer and Narrative

Controls

Your character moves using your iPhone camera. Pointing your MOUTH sideways moves you to the RIGHT or LEFT. Moving your EYEBROW vertically moves you UP or DOWN.

Difficulties

In the hard mode, you play against three enemies at the same time, which turns CUCA CATCH into a very fast and exciting game!

Ingredients

Cuca needs more of some ingredients than others. Pay attention to the REQUEST list.

Events

The event “THE TRAIL” makes it rains a bunch of ingredients in your surroundings, which highly increase your points.
