Pikachu through the year

duration my role team deliveries
1 Month - December of 2019 Digital Designer Lucas Araujo Illustrations and Graphic design

This 2020 calendar was custom-designed for a Pikachu enthusiast, featuring the world’s most iconic Pokémon. Beyond the digital illustration, the project involved the full production cycle, including printing and manual binding. From a functional standpoint, the monthly layouts incorporate lunar phases, specifically integrated to assist the user in tracking her menstrual cycle.
