Challenge your friend in this multiplayer arcade game, dodging bullets from your enemies underwater while competing for treasures with your own submarine.

"Dodging for treasure" is a two-player local competitive multiplayer game. Each of them controls cannons that fire bullets against their opponents while trying to collect treasures in the sea. These treasures may give some advantages or disadvantages for the player who collects than. The player who survives longer wins!
