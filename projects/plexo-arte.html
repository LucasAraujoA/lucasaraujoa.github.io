duration my role team deliveries
4 Months – From February to May of 2019 Graphic Designer and UI Designer Fernanda Netto, Lucas Araujo, Paula Wyller and Matheus Salles Poster, Social networks, Apps, Research and Documentation

We are a (fictitious) brazilian brand that promotes art exhibitions as a way to open discussions about diversity in the contemporary world. An easel and a pad of paper are all you need to be part of these exhibitions and express your opinion through art. We don't have the intention to make money, so all you need to do is access our site and sign up and we will help you in the promotion and production of your art event. Just bring a well-structured theme within the diversity area and leave the rest to us!
